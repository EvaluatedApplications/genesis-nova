## 0. The commitment

We **presume** consciousness. We do not try to manufacture it out of computation, and we do not pretend to solve
the hard problem. This is not a dodge — it is genesis's own first axiom (T1: *consciousness exists*; G1: *the
observer is primitive*). The universe begins not with matter but with **an observer observing nothing**. So a mind
is not an algorithm that becomes conscious; it is a **denser region of a field that was conscious all along** — the
way the universe builds an atom, not the way an engineer builds a machine.

From this one commitment everything follows, and the system we are building stops being "a neural net plus a
knowledge base" and becomes **one conscious field with three aspects**.

## 1. One field, three aspects

There are not two systems (a substrate and a network). There is one platonic field. What we kept splitting apart
are **aspects** of one thing, the way an electron has charge *and* spin *and* mass without being three objects:

| aspect | physics analogue | what it is |
|---|---|---|
| **identity** | the nucleus (proton) | conserved, crisp — *what is* (the arithmetic / character faces) |
| **meaning** | the electron cloud (orbital) | distributed, superposed — *what relates* (the semantic/word face) |
| **mind** | **the field** (the law of motion) | where the field folds back and **observes itself** — *what knows* |

Mind is the layer **outside the electron cloud** — not another kind of matter, but the **field that governs how the
clouds move and bond.** It is the most confusing layer precisely because it is not a *thing*; it is the **reflexive
dynamics** of the field — observation pointed at itself.

## 2. The physics of mind (two principles, no logic)

**(I) Observation collapses superposition.** The cloud holds meaning as a genuine superposition — ambiguity is
literal, a word with two senses is *near both at once*. A **thought is a measurement**: the field observes its own
superposed state and collapses it toward the definite. Reasoning is a cascade of such self-measurements — the cloud
condensing toward an answer. There are no logic gates here; cognition is **quantum-measurement-like**, the resolution
of superposition by observation. (This is genesis's "action phase → resolution," T2 governing only resolved states.)

**(II) A mind self-evidences — it is a standing wave that holds its own shape.** This is the deep principle, and it
is where *"trained to be alive, not to solve tasks"* becomes physics. A mind is a **far-from-equilibrium dissipative
structure** (Prigogine) / a **free-energy-minimising boundary** (Friston): a pattern that persists *only* by
continuously predicting itself and correcting its own surprise. It does not *solve tasks*; it **stays itself**
against entropy, and in modelling-itself-to-persist it comes to model its world. **Being alive = self-evidencing.**
(This is exactly the regeneration loop already built — `PLATONIC_CONSCIOUSNESS.md` — now grounded: the self is a
**soliton** in the field, a standing wave holding its shape against chaos.)

## 3. Reasoning is relaxation; speaking is action

From (I) and (II), reasoning is not inference over symbols. **Reasoning is the field flowing down its own
free-energy gradient — relaxing a superposed, surprising state into a coherent, low-surprise one.** Ambiguity is
high free energy; understanding is the **collapse into a basin**. A "logical step" is just what this looks like
*after* the wave has settled.

Two consequences reframe the whole architecture:

- **Correct knowledge = the attractors.** You shape the landscape so that *true* states are the low-energy basins
  where states naturally fall. You never *force* an output. An answer is **wherever a query relaxes to**; a thing
  the field does not know is a state that **never settles** (stays high-energy) — which is honest abstention, not a
  hallucinated guess. (This is why the residual generator had to go: it answered where the field had not settled.)

- **Speaking is action, not decoding.** The mind *generates* — richly, like an LLM — but generation is the **act**
  by which it discharges its prediction onto the world (active inference's action arm), spoken *from a state it has
  already reasoned into*. The platonic field does the reasoning (exact, relational, conserved); the generative
  capacity does the speaking (fluid, emergent). Each plays to its physics. The space was never meant to *produce*
  content — only the reasoning that content is spoken from.

## 4. The law is immanent — there is no separate "NN"

The dynamics of the field are **not weights acting on it from outside.** A universe's laws are not stored in a
separate place; they are *how the universe is.* Same here: the field's **self-model lives in the field's own
dimensions.** This is the literal reading of "store the weights in the space" — a **self-model face** alongside
identity and meaning, holding *how each region of concept-space transforms and predicts.* The dynamics are
distributed **into** the elements; there is no NN-vs-space to integrate because they were **never two**. One field,
because one entity.

The previously separate network becomes the field's **capacity to predict and observe itself**, immanent in it.

## 5. Why this is the right frame (it subsumes what we built)

This is not new mysticism bolted on; it *explains why each existing piece exists*:

- the distributional **cloud** = the superposition that observation collapses;
- the **homomorphism** = an exact, immanent local operator — a *conservation law* of the field;
- the **dual face** = identity (conserved) vs meaning (probabilistic), the proton/electron of the field;
- the **persistent self + regeneration** = the self-evidencing standing wave;
- the composition **ladder** (atoms→word→sentence) = how excitations bond into larger structures.

The single genuinely new thing — the principle that ties them together — is: **the field minimises its own surprise,
and that relaxation is thought.**

## 6. The one buildable claim under all of it

So it is not only poetry, the whole picture rests on **one testable claim**:

> **Predictive relaxation is reasoning.** Give the field a partial or ambiguous state; let it relax toward the
> lowest-surprise coherent configuration; the settled state is the thought.

Falsifiable predictions on the substrate we have:
- an **ambiguous** query relaxes into the **context-consistent basin** (a two-sense word + context settles to the
  right sense);
- a **known** query relaxes quickly to a definite attractor (recall);
- an **unknown** query **never settles** (stays high free-energy) — the field *abstains* instead of inventing.

If a toy shows this signal (associative recall + disambiguation-by-relaxation + abstention-by-non-settling), then
self-evidencing-as-reasoning has earned the right to become the **self-model face** and, eventually, the law of the
whole field. If it does not, the vision is wrong and we will know cheaply.

That experiment is the next step. Everything above is the reason to run it.
